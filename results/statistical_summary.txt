KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

1. Cohort: 141 cases, 63 deaths, median follow-up 71.5 months
2. Descriptive: Wide heterogeneity in lymphocyte density (CV 162.5%);
   tumour cell density more stable (CV 75.1%)

   tumor r=0.907, both p<0.001)
4. Clinicopathologic: NO significant associations found for any metric
   with STAS, pN, Stage, LVI, or VPI (all p>0.30)
5. Survival: NO significant difference in OS by high/low dichotomisation
   for any metric (all log-rank p>0.50); univariate Cox all p>0.65
6. Multivariable: Digital pathology variables did not improve the base
   clinical model (C-index 0.640 vs 0.631; digital vars p>0.70)
